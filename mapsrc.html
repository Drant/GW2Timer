Daily Achievements

The Dailies Calendar is updated after reset. Hover over an icon to see its achievement name.

Events

Hover over the icon on the map pane and checkmark the option to show Dynamic Events. Then, hover over the icon to view the list of zones sorted by their regions. Click a zone name under the region of today's Event Completer to show where events can happen and find them in game. Alternatively you can do world bosses for their high number of chained events.

Vista

Hover over the icon on the map pane and checkmark the option to display World Completion icons. The fastest vista to view should be the ones next to asura gates in cities.

Gathering

Open the Resource Nodes section of this page to show rich metal nodes or plant farms. Nodes in your home instance (regardless of your character's race) counts as region gatherer: Divinity's Reach is Kryta, Black Citadel is Ascalon, Hoelbrak is Shiverpeaks, Rata Sum and The Grove are Maguuma Jungle.

PvP Dailies
Open the PvP window and click on the Game Browser tab. Look for rooms that say "dailies"; they are specifically for completing dailies and you do not have to fight. You can complete the "Reward" and "Rank" dailies instantly by consuming the potion received from daily rewards. For "Defender", you need to get a kill while the capture point is fully captured by your color.

Collectible Items

Many achievements require you to go around the world to collect or interact with scattered or hidden landmarks or items. These are some of them, including the current Living Story ones if available. When in game, hold the Ctrl key to see the item's name appear. Use the overlay GPS program for easier search.

Every type of collectible is its own checklist. The map markers can be clicked three times to alternate between three ring colors representing an Unfound, Tracked, and Found state, and your address bar will be updated with a shareable URL for those marked. Click Reset for all the rings of that collectible type to be Unfounded again.

Trading Tracker

The basic calculator below does +-*/ operations and accepts parentheses wrapping. You can press the ↑ and ↓ arrow keyboard keys to go through your expression history.

Example expression: 66+(250*8)-125/5
Then press Enter or the = button to perform it.


Each of the row below is a calculator, hover over them to see input (black shaded) and output (gray shaded) boxes. The topmost calculator contains hoverable tooltips about the boxes' function.

To enter an item into a calculator, click the longest input box, which is the name box, and type your search. A list of results will appear, click on one to accept that item, or continue typing to refine your search. The name box also acts as a search bar--press Enter to go to the charts site, and Esc to clear search results.

Once you have chosen an item, the current buy and sell prices will animate in color. You can either use the calculator by putting in your own buy and sell prices in the boxes below the name box, or get notifications if the item price is lower or higher than the current prices by typing your price next them. The prices have a specific syntax, use periods (.) to separate units:

0.1 = 10
.01 = 1
1 = 1
1.55 = 1 55
2.4.7 = 2 4 7
2..6 = 2 0 6
230.6 = 2 30 60
920.. = 920

All input data are saved automatically. To show more calculators or search results, go to Options page, Trading section. Read the user manual for more information.
