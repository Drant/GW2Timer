Daily Achievements

The Dailies Calendar is updated after reset. Hover over an icon to see its achievement name. Some PVE daily icons can be clicked on to zoom to the best place to complete it.

Events

Hover over the icon on the map pane and checkmark the option to show Dynamic Events. Then, hover over the icon to view the list of zones sorted by their regions. Click a zone name under the region of today's Event Completer to show where events can happen and find them in game. Alternatively you can do world bosses for their high number of chained events.

Vista

Hover over the icon on the map pane and checkmark the option to display World Completion icons. The fastest vista to view should be the ones next to asura gates in cities.

Jumping Puzzles Adventures

Open the Jumping Puzzles section of this page to show where and how to do JPs and adventures. Use the search bar find that daily.

Gathering

Open the Resource Nodes section of this page to show rich ore nodes or plant farms. Nodes in your home instance (regardless of your character's race) counts as region gatherer: Divinity's Reach is Kryta, Black Citadel is Ascalon, Hoelbrak is Shiverpeaks, Rata Sum and The Grove are Maguuma Jungle; nodes in your Guild Hall count as Heart of Maguuma.

Miscellaneous

Activity
Go to the red/purple flag icon in a city to start. These may be time consuming except for Crab Toss.
Mystic Forgesmith
Buy 4 cheapest minor sigils on the Trading Post and use them at the Mystic Forge in Lion's Arch.
WvW Big Spender
Go to the Armor/Weapon Master NPC at the citadel of your borderland and buy the skins (not the armor). Alternatively, replenish your deployable traps or buy food using the Permanent Portable Provisioner.
PvP Dailies
Open the PvP window and click on the Game Browser tab. Look for rooms that say "dailies"; they are specifically for completing dailies and you do not have to fight. You can complete the "Reward" and "Rank" dailies instantly by consuming the potion received from daily rewards.

Resource Nodes

Double click a checkbox to show exclusively that resource. On the map, click a node to hide/unhide and right click to zoom in/out. Prices are lowest sell posting. Please report bad or missing nodes.

Rich and T6 nodes respawn in about 22 hours after gathering; T1-T5 nodes respawn in about 1 hour. You can use alts to re-gather any node. More plants on the wiki.

Notice: The map nodes you clicked to hide will be disordered when the site updates the nodes database.

Collectible Items

Many achievements require you to go around the world to collect or interact with scattered or hidden landmarks or items. These are some of them, including the current Living Story ones if available. When in game, enable the general option Show All Usable Object Names to see the item's name appear. Use the overlay GPS program for easier search.

Every type of collectible is its own checklist. The map markers can be clicked three times to alternate between three ring colors representing an Unfound, Tracked, and Found state, and your address bar will be updated with a shareable URL for those marked. Click Reset for all the rings of that collectible type to be Unfounded again.

To convert the map needles into workable pins, click the collectible's image icon from the list.

Ranger Pets

On the map: Click on a pet icon to cycle its ring color and "check them off"; right click to zoom in/out.
On this list: Click on a pet icon to view that pet only, click again to show all pets; double click to view its wiki. The smaller icons are facts for the pet's unique user-activated skill. Turn on the game option "Show All NPC Names" under General Options to always see pet names in game.

Personal Checklist

You may use account API key to automatically fill in your completed dungeons.

You can disable a checkbox by double clicking it (for dungeon paths you never do), and you can re-enable a checkbox by double clicking the number next to it.

Money sum is daily path reward (26 silver + bonus chest) and does not count Bag of Coins, loot, etc. Each numbered path gives 80+20 tokens. More on dungeon rewards.

The personal checklist is automatically cleared after server reset, unless set otherwise in the Options.
