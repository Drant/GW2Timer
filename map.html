Miscellaneous

Activity
Go to the blue/red flag icon in a city to start. These may be time consuming except for Crab Toss.
Costume Brawl
Ask map chat for brawl, kind people will usually not hit back.
Crafter
Refine materials you will use.
Dungeon
Do Citadel of Flame dungeon Path 1 (Ferrah).
Events (Group/Regional/Mentor)
Do world bosses for profit, or Queensdale events for easiness. Or just ask for events in map chat.
Feast
Buy Rice in Bulk from any master chef in town.
Fractal Runner
Start a level 1 or 10 and enter/exit to Dessa's lab until you get the swamp fractal (only need to finish one fractal).
Laurel Vendor
Teleport to your own borderland and walk south for the vendor. Relog to return to your previous spot.
Leveler
Do a dungeon, which gives about 3/4 full bar of XP.
Mystic Forgesmith
Buy 20 very cheap minor sigils on the Trading Post.
Personal Story
Progress your current character, or create a new human and run straight to the giant earth hands boss.
Recycler
Salvage white and blue named items or light and heavy armors.
Skill Point
Level up or consume your stashed Scrolls of Knowledge.
Story Dungeon
Play on a character that needs it. Caudecus' Manor may be the shortest.
